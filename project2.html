Featured Project

Idiom Insight: Figurative Expression Detector

A smart web app that detects idioms and figurative expressions in any paragraph using NLP and fuzzy matching. Perfect for language learners, teachers, and writers.

Project Highlights

NLP Powered

Uses NLP and FuzzyWuzzy to detect idioms and figurative language in text. The system works efficiently even with complex or lengthy paragraphs.

Explains Meanings

Provides clear explanations and highlights the sentence where each idiom is found. Makes understanding figurative language easy and accessible.

For Learners & Teachers

Ideal for students, teachers, writers, and anyone interested in figurative language. Enhances language learning and teaching with real-world examples.
