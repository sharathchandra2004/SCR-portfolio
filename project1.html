Featured Project

Oral Cancer Detection

An AI-powered solution for early and accurate detection of oral cancer from medical images, helping healthcare professionals save lives through timely diagnosis.

Project Highlights

Deep Learning Model

Uses CNNs to analyze oral images and improve detection accuracy. This approach helps spot cancer at an early stage.

Automated & Non-Invasive

Automates the detection process, enabling early, non-invasive screening and helping to prevent unnecessary invasive procedures.

Clinical Impact

Enables timely intervention and expert screening, even in remote areas. Supports healthcare professionals in making informed decisions.
